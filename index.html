    #ABOUT

    Resonance Analytics solves the present situation of expensive, risky and non-sustainable business intelligence solutions by architecting lean, affordable user interactive interfaces tailored to organisations of any reach, size and industry.

    • With a deluge of data coming at us everyday, accessing information is faster than ever, but does that make it more reliable, would everyone interpret it the same way, and would it be easier to evaluate? In fact, it could be more difficult, since we know more data, but have less time to make sense of it.

      At Resonance Analytics we understand that to leverage the power of data and derive meaningful information, the process has to be a seamless intimate experience between the cognitive unconscious and our visual senses. For that reason we have a firm belief that organisations should have the flexibility and ability of being able to explore their data from any angle and be able to drill down on any question to its basic form.

    • It is why, before any endeavour, we have basic starting points and extract insights with the right level of detail and articulate them to our clients. We accomplish this through designing visualisations that vividly stimulate, emphatize and learn through forming clear narratives.

      Our toolset includes consultation, training and development, assembling multipurpose environment dashboards, compelling interactive visualisations, predictive models, KPIs and Metrics, geospatial concentration maps, market analysis, customer engagement reports, financial planning and interactive dynamic and static designs.

      See how Resonance Analytics can help you communicate your data effectively and your organisation to adopt a data-driven culture by contacting us!

    PROFILE

    Having all the information in the world at our fingertips doesnt make it easier to communicate: it makes it harder.

    From health sciences, education, entertainment, space exploration, to holding it in the palm of our hand. Initially invented as a mechanism of measure, technology, has made itself a need in our lives and through multiple advancements, has reached a point where the rest of our physical senses play catch-up with the cognitive visual perception of our media rich environments, this coupled with a desire to make sense of it, serves as an ever growing cause.

    • Having an innate drive to provide individuals seeking clarity, simplification of large volumes in information, and ultimately context; We aim to provide solutions that ease businesses transitioning into this realm of uncertainty, and constantly seek opportunities that exploit our background in extracting meaningful information from Big Data, structuring it and creating interactive visual models that provide the user with an ability to drill down on issues to its basic form and ask questions that were too complex to query (pun intended) before.

      From Co-Founding a start-up to working with director level decision makers, I have helped clients identify anomalies across breadth and depth on all levels within an organisation. An accomplishment driven by leveraging the power of data and analytics, that enables stakeholders to better manage, strategic, tactical and operational performance and drive a business culture that hinges on fact-based data driven decision making.
